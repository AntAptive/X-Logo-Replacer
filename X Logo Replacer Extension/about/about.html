X Logo Replacer

Version 1.4

A browser extension that replaces the X logo on x.com/twitter.com with whatever you want.


How To Use

Simply enter the text you wish to replace the logo with in the "Text" box.

The extension will automatically save your text if you click out of the extension or enable/disable the extension.

Please note this extension only changes the logo at the top-left of the site and does not work on any subdomains of Twitter (ads.twitter.com, careers.twitter.com, etc.).


Privacy Policy

(the legal mumbo jumbo)

This extension does not share any data with the outside world. Any text submitted through the extension is handled entirely on the user's machine.
This extension searches for the HTML element of the X logo on x.com/twitter.com and replaces it locally. This extension does not change the logo's state anywhere else other than on the user's machine.
